Our Story

Founded with a passion for travel and a deep love for India's rich cultural heritage, Wanderluxe has been helping travelers discover the incredible beauty of Bharat since our inception.

From the sacred ghats of Varanasi to the snow-capped peaks of Manali, from the pristine beaches of Goa to the majestic forts of Rajasthan, we curate unforgettable journeys that blend spirituality, adventure, and luxury.

Our mission is simple: to create meaningful travel experiences that connect you with India's soul while ensuring comfort, safety, and exceptional service every step of the way.
